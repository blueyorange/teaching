# Strong Nuclear Force

- Affects all **nucleons** (protons and neutrons).
- Coulomb force affects only **protons**, and is attractive
- repulsive < 0.5 fm
- attractive > 0.5 - 3fm
- zero > 3 fm

---

# Comparison

|             | Strong nuclear force     | Coulomb force |
| ----------- | ------------------------ | ------------- |
| **range**   | 3 fm                     | &infin;       |
| **affects** | protons **and** neutrons | protons       |
