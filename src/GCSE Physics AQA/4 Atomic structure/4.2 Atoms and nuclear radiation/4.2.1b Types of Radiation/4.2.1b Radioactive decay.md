### Properties of radiation

| type          | Mass   | Charge | Ionising | Stopped by        |
| ------------- | ------ | ------ | -------- | ----------------- |
| alpha &alpha; | 4      | 2      | most     | paper/few cms air |
| beta &beta;   | 1/2000 | -1     | middle   | sheet metal       |
| gamma &gamma; | 0      | 0      | least    | lead              |
| neutron n     | 1      | 0      | fairly   | thick concrete    |
